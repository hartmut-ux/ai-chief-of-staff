# Your Personal AI Chief of Staff — Built for Kimi, Claude & Codex

## Hero

**Stop drowning in tabs. Get one decisive daily briefing.**

Your AI chief of staff reads your inbox, calendar, tasks, and web signals, then tells you exactly what deserves your attention — and drafts the actions you can approve in one click. Runs inside Kimi Code CLI, Claude Code, or Codex.

## Why founders use this

- **Protect your focus.** Morning deep-work time is guarded by default.
- **Never miss revenue risk.** Customer and deal emails are surfaced first.
- **Act without the noise.** Automated marketing and low-value pings are filtered out.
- **Assistant-agnostic.** One engine, three skill wrappers. Switch AI frontends without rebuilding your workflow.

## How it works

1. **Connect your sources** — Gmail, Calendar, Notion, Slack, Telegram, plus any custom API or RSS feed.
2. **Run the skill** (`/chief-of-staff` in Kimi, or `chief-of-staff` in Claude/Codex) or run the engine directly:
   ```bash
   python -m chief_of_staff run --preview
   ```
3. **Review your briefing** — top priorities, inbox highlights, calendar, tasks, signals, proposed actions.
4. **Approve actions** — draft, ask, or auto-send per category.
