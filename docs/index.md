# Your Personal AI Chief of Staff — Built for Kimi Code CLI

## Hero

**Stop drowning in tabs. Get one decisive daily briefing.**

Your AI chief of staff reads your inbox, calendar, tasks, and web signals, then tells you exactly what deserves your attention — and drafts the actions you can approve in one click.

## Why founders use this

- **Protect your focus.** Morning deep-work time is guarded by default.
- **Never miss revenue risk.** Customer and deal emails are surfaced first.
- **Act without the noise.** Automated marketing and low-value pings are filtered out.

## How it works

1. **Connect your sources** — Gmail, Calendar, Notion, Slack, plus any custom API or RSS feed.
2. **Run `/chief-of-staff`** inside Kimi Code CLI.
3. **Review your briefing** — top priorities, inbox highlights, calendar, tasks, signals, proposed actions.
4. **Approve actions** — draft, ask, or auto-send per category.
